TypeTab

This little number grabs a random typeface from Google Web Fonts every time you open a new tab.

Only fonts with more than one type style are considered (a la Better Google Fonts.)

Hints

  • 'New tab.' is editable - just click to enter your own custom text.
  • Click any of the font names to jump to their specimen page on Google Web Fonts.
